Our Story

🎶 When Engineers Meet Their Passion 🎸
Welcome to Melodyy Mitra – where circuits meet chords, and code flows like rhythm. We are a group of engineers who found harmony beyond textbooks and deadlines.

What started as casual jam sessions turned into a movement—a collective of passionate musicians dedicated to creating, performing, and sharing the universal language of music. From soulful acoustic covers to high-energy mashups, from intimate jamming nights to electrifying stage performances—we blend creativity with technical precision.

Melodyy Mitra is more than just a music page; it's a tribute to every engineer who dared to dream beyond the conventional. Dive into our world, feel the vibe, and let the melodies speak.
