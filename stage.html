Past Performances

Relive some of our most memorable performances from the past.

March 1, 2025

Open Mic, HLDC

Our performance at the Open Mic, HLDC was a blend of acoustic classics and contemporary hits. The audience joined us in singing along to familiar tunes, creating an atmosphere of community and musical joy. We premiered our Mashup of Sun raha hai x Chahu mai ya na x Mai agar kahu x Meri mehbooba which received tremendous applause.

February 16, 2025

Cura Tarang

This was the first stage show of Melodyy Mitra in which Anish & Ayush did a great job . This event was organized by the Department of Management Studies, NIT Warangal. Over 100 participated from all across India in different events of CURA'25 and among them we got 2nd position in music competition.

We performed Tum se hi X ek ajnabi haseena se which made the audience sing along and vibe with joy. After this first ever stage show, we gained a lot of confidence and also learnt and gained experience for the next upcoming shows.

Many people including professors and other students praised our work which made us feel proud.
